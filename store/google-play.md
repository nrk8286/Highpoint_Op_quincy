## Short Description

Facility operations, compliance, and team workflows for High Point Residence staff.

## Full Description

High Point Ops is a secure operations hub for facility staff. It connects maintenance, housekeeping, nursing, culinary, activities, inventory, compliance, scheduling, reporting, and executive visibility in one mobile-ready workspace.

Staff can launch work orders, review department queues, track compliance tasks, coordinate daily operations, and access role-based tools from the floor or office. The app is built for High Point Residence and Vantage Senior Care teams.

Access requires an authorized staff account.
