## Description

High Point Ops gives authorized facility staff secure mobile access to daily operations and compliance workflows.

The app brings maintenance, housekeeping, nursing, culinary, activities, inventory, scheduling, reporting, and executive visibility into one operations hub. Team members can review department queues, coordinate work, document activity, and keep compliance tasks moving from the floor or office.

Access requires an authorized High Point Residence staff account.

## Privacy Nutrition Label Draft

Data collected and linked to the user may include:

- Contact info: email or username, if used for staff login.
